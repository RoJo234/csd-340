Hobbies

Traveling

Dan has fallen in love with traveling ever since his trip to Spain in high school. Whenever he has time off work, he makes sure he plans a trip. Be it a trip to France or crossing state lines to try a new restaurant he will go almost anywhere.

Gaming

Ever since his parents got him a Nintendo 64 when he was 7 Dan has loved gaming. When he was a child he played Mario. In his teens he played Halo and World of Warcraft with his friends and more recently he has been playing League of Legends and Counter Strike.
League of Legends (LoL) is a competitive 5 vs 5 game with the main goal being to destroy the other team's nexus before they destroy yours. Counter Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a 5 vs 5 tactical first-person shooter (FPS) where two teams compete to be the first win 16 rounds.

Coding

Dan started coding as a hobby in high school when he was making addons for World of Warcraft in his spare time. He has since pursued coding as his professional career, but he still likes to write code in his spare time. He makes programs that automate repititive tasks or anything that he feels like at the time to put his skills to the test.
